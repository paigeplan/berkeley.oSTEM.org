About Us

oSTEM (out in Science, Technology, Engineering, and Mathematics) is the active UC Berkeley chapter of the national oSTEM organization.

We aim to unite the LGBTQ+ students studying STEM to foster a strong professional and social network and to promote LGBTQ+ awareness within the academic and professional communities.
