About Us

oSTEM stands for Out in Science, Technology, Engineering, and Mathematics. We are the active UC Berkeley chapter of the national oSTEM organization
We aim to unite the LGBTQ+ students studying science, technology, engineering, and math to foster a strong professional and social network and to promote LGBTQ+ awareness within the academic and professional communities.
